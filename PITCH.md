## The Operating Philosophy
**"Reliability > Spectacle."**

We don't want an AI that writes poetry. We want an AI that checks the calendar, drafts the email, verifies the facts, and *waits for approval*.

*   **Deterministic:** It does exactly what you tell it. No surprises.
*   **Constrained:** It operates in "Safe Mode" by default. Actions are circumscribed to discrete, atomic units.
*   **Auditable:** Every action is logged. Every decision is traceable.
*   **Judicious:** We optimize for economics and privacy. We don't burn tokens on trivialities; we route tasks to the right model (local or cloud) for the job.

The Back Office isn't a single bot. It's a system of specialized roles working in concert:

*   **The Chief of Staff (Zaphod):** Your primary interface. He coordinates, executes, and protects your attention.
*   **Comms (Marvin):** Drafts messages, manages your tone, and ensures you sound like *you*.
*   **Intel (Ford):** Research and context. He finds the facts so you don't have to.
